Staircase

How To

  1. Choose the colors you want and cut your strings.
  2. Tie your strings together and tape them to your surface. Leave about an inch to 2 inches to tie your bracelet together at the end.
  3. Take your first string and make a 4 over the remaining string.
  4. Take the end of that string and put it through the loop of the 4.
  5. Pull the string through and this will cause it to wrap around the other strings.
  6. Hold down the other strings and pull your first one all the way up.
  7. Repeat until the knots wrap around like a staircase and your stripe is at the desired length.
  8. Switch out your first string with the next color you want and tie the same knots with it around the rest of the strings.
  9. Once your bracelet has reached the desired length, tie it off and cut any extra string.
  10. Give it to a friend or tie it on your own wrist and you've made a staircase bracelet!
